Instructions:

Your goal is to get the maximum score you can. In order to achieve this, you must avoid getting hit by the jets that will try to fly into you. These are going to get faster along the game.

GOOD LUCK!

Controls:

  • "ARROW UP" - Jump, start new game or unpause.
  • "ARROW DOWN" - Reach the floor faster while on the air (after a jump).
  • "P" - pause / unpause game or start a new one.
